PAZA 4 YOUTH

PAZA 4 YOUTHS is a non-profitable youth-based organization with aims to aid in representing youths in matters that propels the wellbeing and growth of a youth.

We basically want to help the youths' voices be heard and all of their opinions and decisions to be taken into consideration in order to aid in the implementation processes of youth associated programs.
