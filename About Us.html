Our Mission

P4Y is an initiative for and by the young people whose goal is to ensure the voices of young people is heard at all levels in life, work, schools and within the community.

The initiative aims to empower young people to:

  • Increase participation in advocacy for vulnerable youths.
  • Increase demand for access to sexual reproductive health options
  • Improve their quality of life
  • To help seek pool of skilled and confident 'P4Y' champions as change agents who can engage in media related advocacy as well as to contribute to policy and program meetings within county and national levels.

Our Vision

A more just world where every young person can transform their own and others' lives by engaging more youths in life changing matters.
